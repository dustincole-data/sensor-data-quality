This measures data-reporting health (is the feed live, complete, and in-range?), not air quality and not sensor accuracy. Each Sensor is graded 0–100 on three checks — staleness (silent > 24h), completeness (trailing-30-day coverage < 90%), and plausibility (window min < 0 or max > 1000 µg/m³). The headline is the share failing at least one.
